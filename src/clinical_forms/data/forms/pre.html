Open Positions: Branch ExCom

Chair

The Strategist (PMO)

The face of the branch. You manage the overall strategy, lead Executive Committee meetings, and ensure all committees are moving forward.

Treasurer

The Operator

Handles the funding and operations. You aren't just holding money; you ensure fiscal performance and manage the dues cycle.

Open Positions: RAS Chapter

Vice Chair

The right hand to the Chair. Steps in to lead and manages operations and 'Project Squad' meetings.

Treasurer

Manages the specific budget for robotics projects, competitions, and society workshops.

Secretary

The backbone of documentation. Ensures the "5 Ws" (Who, What, Where, When, Why) are communicated.
